A Part of Zermatt

The Mont Cervin Palace is part of Zermatt – and Zermatt is part of the Mont Cervin Palace. This special connection has grown over 160 years. No cars, just tranquillity, clean air and idyllic mountains, all concentrated in the smallest of spaces. From the Bahnhofsstrasse, it is just a stone's throw through the romantic old village centre to the great outdoors. And rising above it all is the Matterhorn.

Unobtrusive luxury

All the comforts of a private residence, all the variety of a resort. Outside the stately appearance, inside the warm and familiar atmosphere – 5-star luxury meets home comforts in the ultimate location at the heart of Zermatt.

Feel like home.

Experiences at the foot of a glacier, on a green alpine meadow, at an idyllic mountain lake. The air is crystal clear. The view of the world-famous Matterhorn. The windows of the Mont Cervin Palace cast a warm glow against the darkening skies. You enter our hotel, and our staff warmly welcomes you.
